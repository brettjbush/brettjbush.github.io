Music

Adventure Level - Sample

This work strives to evoke feelings of adventure, excitement, and exploration to accompany a large sandbox style of level.

The theme of Gusty Garden Galaxy from Super Mario Galaxy is a strong inspiration for this piece and my goal is to match that same level of exhilaration and excitement.

Battle Theme - Sample

The piece takes inspiration from early Final Fantasy battle theme songs by Nobuo Uematsu.

The combination of rich strings, hectic piano, and a driving beat seek to throw the listener right into the midst of a thrilling battle. The Dies Irae motif is incorporated in as well to give an additional sense of gravity, see if you can find it!

Shop Theme - Sample

An easy, breezy melody to accompany your local small town NPC shopkeeper as they bop side to side in their idle animation waiting to sell or buy items on your quest.

The piece is reminiscent of the Onett Drug Store from Earthbound with the old time jazz feel one would find in the soundtrack of the kids show Max and Ruby.
